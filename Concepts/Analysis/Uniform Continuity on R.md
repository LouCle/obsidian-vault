> [!INFO] Definition
> A function $f : A \subset \mathbb R^n \to \mathbb R^m$ is uniformly continuous on $B\subseteq A$ if $$\forall \epsilon>0 ~\exists \delta>0~~~ d(f(x),f(y)) < \epsilon~~~ x,y\in B~\mathrm{with}~d(x,y)<\delta.$$
>
